TUNNEL OF SORROW: CONCLUSIONS
==============================

GOAL
----
Predict the maximum intraday SPX move (from open) using the VIX opening level.
Use this to set expected move bands for day trading.


THE FORMULA
-----------
Given VIX at the open, the expected max SPX % move from open is:

  move% = 0.1953 + 0.0659 * (VIX - 5) + 0.000440 * (VIX - 5)^2

This is a 75th percentile quadratic quantile regression, fit on 5,583 trading
days from 2004-2026. It contains 75% of observed daily max moves.

To convert to a decimal multiplier for price levels:

  move  = 0.001953 + 0.000659 * (VIX - 5) + 0.00000440 * (VIX - 5)^2
  top   = SPX_open * (1 + move)
  bottom = SPX_open * (1 - move)


WHY THIS FORMULA
----------------
1. VIX - 5 (zero-basing): The regression x-intercept is 5.0 across multiple
   data subsets. Subtracting 5 eliminates the negative intercept and makes the
   model proportional -- at VIX = 5, expected move = ~0%.

2. Quadratic term (vol-of-vol): At low VIX, residual scatter is tight (std
   0.33% at VIX 10-15). At high VIX, it fans out 6x (std 2.10% at VIX 50+).
   The (VIX-5)^2 term widens the band at elevated VIX to account for this.
   Without it, the model underpredicts tail moves in crisis regimes.

3. 75th percentile: The mean prediction only contains ~59% of days. The 75th
   percentile contains 75% -- a better threshold for trading bands where you
   want most days to stay inside. For tighter or wider bands:

     90th: 0.2947 + 0.0874*(VIX-5) + 0.000531*(VIX-5)^2  [contains 90%]
     95th: 0.4042 + 0.0970*(VIX-5) + 0.000806*(VIX-5)^2  [contains 95%]

4. 2004+ data: R^2 improves from 0.447 (full sample) to 0.493 (2004+) by
   dropping pre-decimalization 1990s data. The slope is stable post-2000,
   and the 2004+ window still includes GFC and COVID stress tests.

Mental math shortcut (linear approximation):
  move% ~= 0.07 * (VIX - 5)
  Every 1 point of VIX above 5 adds ~7 bps of expected max move.
  VIX 20 = ~1.05%. VIX 30 = ~1.75%. VIX 40 = ~2.45%.
  Then add ~20-30% buffer at VIX 30+ for vol-of-vol.


VS. THE ORIGINAL TOS SCRIPT
----------------------------
Old: move = 0.000609 * (VIX - 8) + 0.0037
New: move = 0.001953 + 0.000659 * (VIX - 5) + 0.00000440 * (VIX - 5)^2

The old formula is ~13% too flat at high VIX and uses a floor of 8 instead
of the data-derived 5. It contains ~65% of daily moves. The new formula
matches at normal VIX (15-25), is slightly tighter at low VIX, and widens
appropriately at high VIX where the old formula was too narrow.
